Interactive Elements

In-text link

CSS selector:

a

Sample code:

<a href="https://www.youtube.com/">Go to Youtube</a>

Rendered element:

Go to Youtube

Navigation Link

CSS selector:

.button-nav

Sample code:

<a href="#About"><i class="fa fa-user fa-fw"></i>About</a> <a href="BiancaPricop_Resume.pdf" target="_blank"><i class="fa fa-download fa-fw"></i>Resume</a>

Rendered element:

About

Resume

Bio

I'm currently studying Interactive Systems and Design in the Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) program at Simon Fraser University concentrating in design and interactive systems. Prior to studying at SFU, I studied Business and Commerce at Douglas College for over 2 years which taught me practical business knowledge that can be applied to SIAT.

My goal is to create aesthetically pleasing designs and experiences for websites and applications. Creating enjoyable experiences for people and making an impact when users interact with my work is important for me. I strive to keep learning and improving my skills to work in the UX/UI design field.
